Advice for employeers during COVID-19 crisis

The team could set up an always on video call, where you’ll just hang out, do your work and “disturb” each other with questions – basically as you do when you’re around your desk. It has the added benefit of being able to share your screen when you have those types of questions.

Advice for employees during COVID-19 crisis

Evaluate what you need to work properly. Having to move around the entire house breaks your concentration, which can cause you to feel less productive and frustrated. As best you can, separate your workspace from your home to avoid the feeling that you’re always at work.
